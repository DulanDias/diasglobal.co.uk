How This Calculator Works

This calculator uses the current Sri Lanka PAYE tax system and statutory deductions:

  • Tax-Free Allowance: LKR 1,800,000 per year (first 1,800,000 is tax-free)
  • PAYE Tax Rates: Progressive rates from 6% to 36% based on income brackets
  • EPF (Employee Provident Fund): 8% of gross salary - mandatory retirement savings
  • ETF (Employee Trust Fund): 3% of gross salary - mandatory retirement savings

Tax Brackets (2025):

  • • 6% on first LKR 1,000,000 of taxable income (LKR 1,800,001 - 2,800,000)
  • • 18% on next LKR 500,000 (LKR 2,800,001 - 3,300,000)
  • • 24% on next LKR 500,000 (LKR 3,300,001 - 3,800,000)
  • • 30% on next LKR 500,000 (LKR 3,800,001 - 4,300,000)
  • • 36% on remaining income (above LKR 4,300,000)

Note: Tax rates and allowances may change. This calculator provides estimates based on current regulations. For official tax calculations, consult with the Inland Revenue Department of Sri Lanka.
